STEPS TO GET THE OPEN WEATHER API KEY :
STEP 01: Open the OpenWeather website.
(Link: https://home.openweathermap.org)

STEP 02: Click the “Sign in” button.

STEP 03: Create an Account in the portal.

STEP 04: Click on your username, and a dropdown menu appears.

Click the “My API” option.

STEP 05: Select the key and copy it.
